Claude Has a Connector for Everything — And I Used One to Pull 100 Real Leads in Minutes

Here's how the Vibe Prospecting connector inside Claude eliminates the hardest part of growing your business — and how you can set it up in seven steps.

Published March 5, 2026
Key Takeaway

Claude's Connectors feature integrates directly with third-party tools — including Vibe Prospecting, a B2B data platform with access to 150 million companies and 800 million professionals. Using this connector inside Claude, I pulled a targeted list of 100 small business owners in New York's retail and food service industries in under two minutes, with LinkedIn profiles and company data included. No spreadsheets. No switching tabs.

Claude Literally Has a Connector for Everything — And That Changes Everything

One of the things that genuinely excites me about where Claude is right now is the Connectors feature. We're not just talking about a chatbot that answers questions anymore. Claude now integrates with external platforms — tools you're already using or should be using — and brings their full power directly into your conversation.

Canva. Gmail. Google Calendar. Meta Ads. And now, Vibe Prospecting.

You don't have to leave the chat. You don't have to open a new dashboard, pull a CSV, or re-import anything. You just talk to Claude in plain English, and it identifies that you want to use the connector, handles the connection in the background, and delivers your results right there in the conversation.

The Hardest Problem in Business Just Got Solved

What's the thing that stops most people from growing their business? It's not the product. It's not the service. It's not even the pitch. It's finding the right people to pitch to.

Who is my ideal customer? Where do I find them? How do I get in front of them without spending thousands on ads or buying cold email lists that don't convert?

That problem — the lead generation problem — has pretty much been eliminated with this connector. I used it to pull 100 small business owners in New York, specifically in retail and food service. I got their names, job titles, company names, locations, and LinkedIn profiles. I didn't have to build a spreadsheet. I didn't have to scrub a list. I just told Claude what I needed, and it delivered.

Dr. Erin's Analysis

What we're watching right now is the collapse of the distance between thinking and doing. In the past, lead generation required a separate tool, a separate workflow, a separate skill set — you needed to know how to use Apollo or ZoomInfo or LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and you needed time. Now, that entire process lives inside a conversation. You describe your ideal prospect in plain English, and Claude does the work. I've tested this myself. It works. The barrier to entry for finding and pitching your dream clients just dropped dramatically, and the people who recognize that first will move faster than everyone else.

How to Set Up the Vibe Prospecting Connector in Claude: Step-by-Step

This is not sponsored content. There is no affiliate link. I'm sharing this because I've used it, I think it's valuable, and I believe in sharing tools that actually work with this community. Vibe Prospecting offers a free trial — no credit card required.

  1. Go into your Claude settings.
  2. Click on Connectors.
  3. Search for Vibe Prospecting in the connector library.
  4. Click to make the connection — you'll be prompted to create a Vibe Prospecting account. Free trial, no credit card required.
  5. Sign up and complete the connection back in Claude.
  6. Once in the chat, speak naturally — tell Claude what leads you need. It will recognize you want the connector.
  7. Claude will ask for your permission at each step. Approve and watch it go.
Bottom Line

Claude's Connectors feature turns your AI chat into a full lead generation engine. With Vibe Prospecting connected, you can pull a targeted list of business owners, founders, or decision-makers in any industry and location — directly inside Claude, in minutes. No extra tools. No manual research. Free trial, no credit card required. If you've been struggling to find the right people to pitch your services to, this is the tool that changes that workflow for good.
