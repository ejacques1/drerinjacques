The 4 videos

What each one does.

Video 1

Connect your domain

Point the domain you bought in Week 2 to your live platform, and update your Supabase URLs at the same time so logins and confirmation emails work on the real site instead of localhost.

By the end: your platform lives at your own web address, not a Vercel link.
Video 2

Take payments (Stripe)

Connect Stripe and set your pricing (monthly vs annual, lead with monthly for your MVP), then wire subscriptions into the login you already built. The Stripe MCP lets Claude set up your product, price, and coupon by conversation.

By the end: you can charge money, and only paying members get in.
Video 3

Opt-ins & email marketing

Set up your funnel and email platform, then build the flow: lead magnet → opt-in → tag → automated follow-up. Bring your whole existing email list in.

By the end: you can capture leads and follow up with them automatically.
Video 4

Test a real signup, end to end

Sign up on your live domain as a real user. Confirm the email lands and redirects to your real site, the account shows in your database, and a real test payment runs through Stripe.

By the end: you've proven a real person can go landing → signup → payment → access. That's launch.
⚠️ A green build proves nothing. The only proof is a real person completing the full flow on the real domain.
