AI can generate code. It cannot make founder decisions. It cannot choose the right architecture. It cannot determine which features matter. It cannot tell you how to structure your database, handle customer onboarding, process payments, or prepare your platform for real users.

That's why so many people start building with AI and never actually launch.

The problem isn't the coding. The problem is everything around the coding.

Between Sessions

You're not coding alone between sessions. Instead, you'll prepare for the next session by:

  • Drafting content (landing copy, onboarding emails)
  • Setting up accounts (Claude Pro, GitHub, Vercel, Supabase, Stripe)
  • Testing what we've built as a user

All technical building happens during our live sessions together.
